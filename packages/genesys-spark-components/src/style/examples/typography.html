Typography

Text Styles

The component library enables the use of Spark typography. The typography styles are decoupled from the semantic meaning of HTML elements such as h1, h2, p etc. They can be mixed and matched based on the desired outcome or information architecture using tokens or utility classes.

Headlines & Subheads

Urbanist will be used for Headers for English and the additional 13 languages supported by Urbanist. For the remaining languages Noto Sans will be used for header and body copy.
